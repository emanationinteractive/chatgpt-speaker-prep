  YOUR DATA — Speaker Prep v1.0
  This folder is YOURS. Put your stuff here.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

This is your personal filing cabinet. The tool
(Speaker Prep) lives in the other zip. This zip
is where YOUR content goes as you build your talk.

Nothing here gets sent anywhere. It's just folders
on your machine for you to stay organized.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════

FOLDER MAP:

  my_speaker_data/my_notecards/
    Save notecards here as you go. When the AI
    shows "saved notecard 007: your_slug" — copy
    that text into a file here. Name it whatever.
    This is your backup. The AI chat will lose them
    when the session ends. This folder won't.

  my_speaker_data/my_outline/
    When you run "outline" and like what you see,
    copy-paste it into a file here. Save versions.
    outline_v1.txt, outline_v2.txt, etc.

  my_speaker_data/my_comfort_cards/
    Your stage comfort techniques. Body stuff.
    "Look at the back wall." "Sip water = reset."
    Keep these separate because they're about YOU,
    not about your talk content.

  my_speaker_data/my_cue_sheet/
    Your emoji-to-notecard mappings for live talks.
    Run "sheet" in the tool, copy the output here.
    Print it. Put it on your phone. Tape it to
    your laptop lid. Whatever works.

  my_speaker_data/raw_dumps/
    Brain dumps, meeting notes, Discord convos,
    email threads — anything you want to paste
    into the "parse" command later. Save the raw
    text here BEFORE you paste it, so you have
    a copy if the AI session dies.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════

WORKFLOW:

  1. Prep session → notecards come out → save to my_speaker_data/my_notecards/
  2. Good outline? → save to my_speaker_data/my_outline/
  3. Stage tips? → save to my_speaker_data/my_comfort_cards/
  4. Ready for live talk? → save cue sheet to my_speaker_data/my_cue_sheet/
  5. Got raw messy notes? → save to my_speaker_data/raw_dumps/ then paste into "parse"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════

STARTING A NEW SESSION:

  When you open a new chat with your AI, you need to reload.
  Two options:

  QUICK: Paste 2_speaker_prep_prompt.txt from the tool zip.
         Then paste your saved notecards back in with "notes".

  FULL:  Paste LOAD_ALL.txt from the tool zip (loads everything
         in one shot). Then paste your saved notecards back in.

  Your data is always here. The AI forgets between sessions.
  This folder remembers.
